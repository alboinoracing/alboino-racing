  • Where do we come from?

    Our starting point is the Canossian Institute of Feltre BL, in Veneto and we study in a STEM high school (sience, technology, engineering, mathematics,), an applied scientific school, with a particular approach to study subjects. Our school still equality treated no matter the religion.

What is a STEM week?

What distinguishes our school among others is the STEM week, a week that doesn't include normal studies and is completely dedicated to a single project chosen by the teachers. During this week we must commit ourselves to create the best possible project and then show it to middle school students and judges who they will decide which one will be best.

  • What is Formula 1 in schools?

    Every year Formula 1 hosts a competition where students from all over the world compete to create the fastest, most aerodynamic, and finally aesthetic car. Doing so, however, is not easy task as a meticulous regulation must be followed which requires the car to have specific dimensions, weights, and the use of specific programs, which requires a level of unexpected time and effort.

Our history

As we were choosing groups for STEM week, as soon as this project was named, he enthusiasts came out and immediately got to work. First thing first, we had to decide on an icon for our team this is when we thought about the Alboino Castle, iconic of our city of origin, Feltre. Afterwards we decided on the roles we would specialize in: the mechanics or creation of the model; aerodynamics; marketing to find sponsors and manage the economic part; other minor things like the creation of themed suits or posters.
